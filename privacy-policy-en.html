Trovel Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-20 · Effective: 2026-05-20

Hasegi Inc. ("we") values your privacy and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act of the Republic of Korea and relevant laws. This policy applies to the mobile application "Trovel" (Korean title: 방구석 세계여행, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

The Service does not require sign-up, so we do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, or phone number. The following data is processed automatically:

TypeDetailsStorage
Visit recordsIDs of landmarks marked as visited, stamp dates, streak countsLocal device (SharedPreferences)
Notification settingsWhether the daily reminder is enabled and at what timeLocal device (SharedPreferences)
Onboarding stateWhether the first-run intro was completedLocal device (SharedPreferences)
Network request logsStandard HTTP requests generated when fetching Wikipedia images / summariesWikipedia (external) — we do not retain

2. Purposes

  • Preserve visit records and stamp progress (locally on device)
  • Send the daily "Landscape of the Day" reminder (only when enabled by the user)
  • Improve service quality and statistical analysis

3. External Service (Wikipedia)

The Service fetches landmark photos and summary text from the Wikimedia Foundation's public Wikipedia REST API. During this process, standard HTTP request information such as your IP address and device metadata may be transmitted to Wikimedia servers. We do not collect or retain this information separately. Wikimedia's privacy policy is available at foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Policy:Privacy_policy.

4. Notification Permission

The Service may request notification permission to deliver the daily "Landscape of the Day" reminder. You may decline this permission or revoke it at any time in your system settings. Notifications are produced entirely as local notifications on your device. We do not send push notifications from our servers.

5. Advertising

The Service currently displays no advertising. If advertising is introduced in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide in-app notice.

6. In-App Purchases

The Service currently offers no paid in-app purchases. If paid items are introduced in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Service accordingly.

7. Retention

We do not retain personally identifiable information on our servers. Visit records, notification settings and other local data are removed when you uninstall the app.

8. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties, except as required by law or by legitimate requests from investigative authorities.

9. User Rights

You may revoke notification permission at any time in your system settings, and all visit records and local data are removed immediately when the app is uninstalled. For additional rights, contact us below.

10. Children Under 14

The Service is available to children under 14, but since we do not collect identifiable personal information, no separate guardian consent process is implemented.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Policy in response to legal or service changes; revisions take effect upon posting on this page.
